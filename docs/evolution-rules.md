# Evolution Rules

## Overview

Every creature has a base form at hatching. Evolution is triggered by sustained environmental conditions, bond rating, or specific item combinations. Creatures cannot evolve immediately — they require a minimum of 24 hours in stable conditions before the first evolution is possible.

## Shadow Evolution

- **Abyssian form**: Expose to total darkness (light < 50) for 12 consecutive hours. The creature's void abilities intensify and it gains phasing capability.
- **Phantom form**: Reach 80% bond rating and feed moonlight-infused crystals. The creature becomes partially incorporeal.

## Moon Evolution

- **Selene form**: Wait for a full moon cycle (or simulate with sustained dim light for 48 hours). The creature's gravity abilities become active rather than passive.
- **Celestial form**: Bond rating must reach 75%. The creature's dream-manipulation abilities extend to waking states.

## Solar Evolution

- **Stellarion form**: Achieve sustained intense light exposure (light > 900) for 3 consecutive days. The creature can generate its own light independent of the environment.
- **Celestial Ember form**: Requires a Solar Stone item. The creature gains heat resistance and hybrid fire-light abilities.

## Bloom Evolution

- **Arborian form**: Daily moderate light and consistent watering (humidity > 50%) for 7 days. The creature grows significantly in size and gains bark-like armour.
- **Ancient Grove variant**: Feed rare pollen (high-humidity environment for 72 hours). Unlocks deep-root abilities and terrain manipulation.

## Aquatic Evolution

- **Abyssal form**: Sustain humidity above 80% for 48 consecutive hours. Deep-sea abilities emerge — pressure resistance, sonar, and bioluminescence.
- **Leviathan variant**: Bond with 3 other Aquatic creatures (hatch 3 Aquatic-type creatures in the same session). The creature grows a second form with area-of-effect water abilities.

## Ember Evolution

- **Inferno form**: Raise ambient temperature above 35°C for 6 consecutive hours. The creature's Heat Aura becomes an active attack and Magma Skin fully hardens.
- **Volcarius form**: Combine with a Lava Crystal item during a high-temperature period. Grants explosive abilities and terrain-scorching attacks.

## Nature Evolution

- **Ancient Nature form**: Spend 24 hours in a balanced outdoor environment (all sensors within normal range simultaneously). Unlocks ancient vine and root-network abilities.
- **World Tree variant**: Achieve harmony across all biome types by hatching one creature of every type in the same session. The Nature creature gains elemental affinity for all types.

## Bond Rating

Bond rating increases with each interaction (button press) recorded during and after hatching. It caps at 100% and does not reset between sessions. Higher bond rating improves personality traits and unlocks certain evolution paths.

## General Rules

- A creature can only evolve once unless a special item is used.
- Evolution resets the hatching display but retains all previous data in history.
- The LED colour shifts during evolution to indicate the new form's element.
