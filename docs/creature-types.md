# Creature Types

## Shadow

Shadow creatures emerge from very low-light environments (below 200/1023 lux reading). They are mysterious, cautious, and often solitary. Shadow-types possess stealth and illusion abilities, with mastery over darkness and void energy. They are nocturnal by nature and uncomfortable in direct sunlight. Rarity skews toward Uncommon and above.

Core abilities: Shadow Step, Void Cloak, Nightmare Veil

## Moon

Moon creatures develop in dim environments (light 200–400/1023). They are calm, introspective, and patient. Moon-types are drawn to cyclical patterns and have abilities related to gravity, dreams, and subtle manipulation. They are strategic rather than aggressive.

Core abilities: Moonbeam, Tidal Pull, Dream Weave

## Bloom

Bloom creatures emerge in bright but not extreme light (600–800/1023). They are nurturing, social, and growth-oriented. Bloom-types are deeply tied to plant life and photosynthesis. They thrive in warm, sunlit conditions and heal rapidly.

Core abilities: Petal Storm, Root Grasp, Photosynthetic Heal

## Solar

Solar creatures require intense light exposure (above 800/1023). They are the rarest light-type, energetic and bold. Solar-types command radiant energy, capable of blinding bursts and sustained beam attacks. Their power scales directly with light intensity at time of hatching.

Core abilities: Solar Flare, Photon Charge, Sunbeam Lance

## Aquatic

Aquatic creatures form in high-humidity environments (above 70%). They are peaceful, fluid, and adaptable. Aquatic-types control water and mist, with defensive and healing abilities. They are among the most common types globally but rare in dry environments.

Core abilities: Tidal Wave, Hydro Shield, Rain Call

## Ember

Ember creatures are born of heat (temperature above 30°C). They are fierce, passionate, and territorial. Ember-types command fire and heat, dealing sustained damage and resisting physical attacks through their hardened exterior. They are aggressive when threatened.

Core abilities: Ember Burst, Heat Aura, Magma Skin

## Nature

Nature creatures emerge under balanced environmental conditions — moderate light, temperature, and humidity without extremes. They are the most common type and the most versatile. Nature-types adapt to many environments and have utility-focused abilities rather than specialised elemental power.

Core abilities: Vine Whip, Camouflage, Nature's Pulse
