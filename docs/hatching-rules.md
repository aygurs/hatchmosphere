# Hatching Rules

## Light Conditions

Creatures that hatch in very low light (below 200/1023) tend to develop stealth abilities and nocturnal habits. Shadow-type eggs require near-total darkness to fully develop. Prolonged exposure to darkness strengthens void-based and illusion abilities.

High solar exposure (above 800/1023) during incubation produces Solar or Bloom-type creatures with radiant energy abilities. The longer and more intense the light exposure, the more powerful the creature's energy output becomes.

Dim ambient light (400–600/1023) triggers Moon-phase development, encouraging calm, introspective personalities and gravity or dream-related abilities.

## Temperature Conditions

Temperature extremes during incubation lock in elemental traits. Creatures incubated above 30°C almost always develop fire or heat-based abilities. Those incubated above 35°C may exhibit volcanic or magma traits.

Cold incubation (below 5°C) can intensify Shadow or Moon traits. Balanced temperatures (18–25°C) are ideal for Nature-type development.

## Humidity Conditions

High humidity (above 70%) creates aquatic incubation conditions. Sustained humidity above 85% may produce rarer Aquatic variants with deep-sea abilities.

Very low humidity (below 15%) encourages desert or arid traits, which can shift Ember creatures toward stronger fire tolerance.

## Interaction During Incubation

Creatures that experience frequent interaction (5+ button presses) during incubation develop social, curious personalities. They bond more quickly with their caretakers and may gain bonus personality traits.

Very high interaction (10+ presses) can push rarity upward — the creature senses the care given and develops more complex traits as a result.

## Rarity Factors

Rarity is determined by a combination of environmental extremity and caretaker engagement. Each extreme condition (light, temperature, humidity outside normal ranges) contributes to rarity score. Interaction count adds bonus points. A small random factor ensures no two hatches are identical.

Final rarity tiers: Common → Uncommon → Rare → Ultra Rare → Legendary
