# Biomes

## Shadowfell Biome

A cold, lightless realm deep underground. Shadow and Moon creatures originate here. Characterised by near-zero ambient light, high humidity, and perpetual fog. Temperatures range from 5–15°C. The ecosystem is sustained by bioluminescent fungi and thermal vents rather than sunlight. Shadow creatures born in the deepest zones have the most powerful void abilities.

Associated creature types: Shadow, Moon
Trigger conditions: light < 200, temperature < 15

## Lunar Highlands

Silvery plateaus at high altitude, bathed in soft reflected moonlight rather than direct sunlight. Moon creatures thrive here. The environment is quiet, with thin air and predictable seasonal cycles. Gravity-manipulation abilities are more pronounced in creatures born near the highland peaks.

Associated creature types: Moon
Trigger conditions: light 200–400, moderate temperature and humidity

## Solar Expanse

High-altitude cliffs with unobstructed sunlight for most of the day. The most intense naturally-lit environment in the known world. Solar and Bloom creatures born here are energetic and fast-growing. UV exposure during hatching amplifies radiant energy abilities significantly.

Associated creature types: Solar, Bloom
Trigger conditions: light > 800

## Sunlit Meadows

Warm, open fields with rich fertile soil and consistent moderate-to-bright light. The most common biome for Bloom creatures. Temperatures are stable (20–30°C) and humidity is moderate. Ideal for growth-based and healing-oriented creatures.

Associated creature types: Bloom, Nature
Trigger conditions: light 600–800, temperature 18–28

## Mist Marshes

Deep, perpetually humid wetlands with warm, misty waters. Aquatic creatures are most at home here. The air is thick with moisture, and surface visibility is low. Water temperature is warmer than most aquatic environments, encouraging a diverse ecosystem.

Associated creature types: Aquatic
Trigger conditions: humidity > 70

## Ember Wastes

A harsh volcanic biome with geothermal vents, lava fields, and extreme ambient heat. Only Ember creatures and a handful of heat-adapted species survive here. Temperatures regularly exceed 40°C. Creatures born in this biome have the most aggressive temperaments.

Associated creature types: Ember
Trigger conditions: temperature > 30

## Verdant Forest

Balanced ecosystems with moderate climate, mixed light, and stable humidity. The most common and widespread biome. Nature creatures are born here in great numbers. The diversity of conditions means Nature creatures are generalists — capable in many environments but masters of none.

Associated creature types: Nature
Trigger conditions: balanced light, temperature, humidity — no extremes
